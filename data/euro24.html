Who needs what?

Slovakia will be through to the round of 16, at least as a best third-place team, if they win. They'll be confirmed in the top two in Group E if they win and Belgium do not beat Romania on Saturday. Ukraine will be unable to finish in the top two if they lose to Slovakia and Belgium do not beat Romania.

Ukraine make a change

The experienced Stepanenko is replaced in key holding midfield role by 22-year-old Brazhko for Ukraine.

Duda returns too

Ondrej Duda is a very familiar face to Bundesliga fans - the Slovakia midfielder spent seven years with Hertha Berlin and Cologne. "Three years ago, we got knocked out after our first win, but that's football. It can be cruel," he said in the pre-match presser. Will it be cruel (or kind) to Duda & Co. today?
